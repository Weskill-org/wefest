WEFEST
End-to-End College Festival & Event Management Ecosystem
A college-first, identity-based, closed-network platform for hosting, managing, sponsoring, and monetizing college festivals.
________________________________________
1️⃣ VISION
To become the official digital infrastructure layer for college festivals in India (and globally) — managing everything from planning to sponsorship to ticketing to analytics.
WeFest will not just be an event app.
It will be:
•	🎓 A college-specific event ecosystem
•	🏢 A sponsor discovery + analytics platform
•	🎟 A ticketing + verification engine
•	👥 A society/team management system
•	📊 A data intelligence engine for brands and colleges
________________________________________
2️⃣ CORE PROBLEM WE ARE SOLVING
🎓 For Colleges
•	Fragmented event management (WhatsApp, Excel, Google Forms)
•	No sponsor discovery infrastructure
•	No centralized ticketing system
•	No post-event analytics
•	No digital archive of past fests
👥 For Students
•	No centralized place to see college events
•	Fake/duplicate ticket scams
•	No smooth payment or verification
•	No unified college fest identity
🏢 For Companies
•	No structured discovery of college fests
•	No verified footfall analytics
•	No demographic targeting
•	No ROI measurement for campus sponsorships
________________________________________
3️⃣ PLATFORM ARCHITECTURE
WeFest will operate on a College-Based Multi-Tenant Model.
Each user MUST select a college at signup.
They will see:
•	Only their college events
•	Or National/Open fests (if marked as open to all)
This builds:
✅ Identity-based exclusivity
✅ Controlled event access
✅ Campus network effect
