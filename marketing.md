# WeFest Marketing Strategy & Roadmap

## 1. SEO & Trust Infrastructure (Immediate Priority)

### Legal Pages Implementation
To establish platform trust and meet compliance standards, we will implement the following pages, linked from the global footer:
- **Terms of Service**: Detailed platform usage guidelines, ticketing policies, and user responsibilities.
- **Privacy Policy**: GDPR-compliant data handling, student identity protection, and cookie usage.
- **Refund Policy**: Clear rules on ticket cancellations, event postponements, and organizer liabilities.
- **Cookie Policy**: Transparency on tracking and personalization.

### SEO Optimization
- **Dynamic Meta Tags**: Implement unique `<title>`, `<meta description>`, and OpenGraph tags for every event page.
- **Schema Markup**: Add JSON-LD schema for Events and Organizations to appear in Google Search "rich results".
- **Semantic HTML**: Ensure proper H1-H6 hierarchy across all landing pages.

## 2. Content & Authority Strategy

### The WeFest Blog ("The Campus Pulse")
Develop a high-authority blog with 14+ SEO-optimized articles targeting the following pillars:
- **Event Mastery**: "How to plan a college fest on a budget", "Winning premium sponsors".
- **Sponsorship ROI**: Deep dives into data-driven sponsorship metrics.
- **Student Spotlight**: Talent discovery and student leadership features.
- **Innovation & Tech**: AI in event management and digital infrastructure.
- **Cultural Significance**: The evolution of Indian college fests.

### Viral Loops
- **"Share to Earn"**: Incentivize students to share events on WhatsApp/Instagram for small discounts or exclusive access.
- **Leaderboards**: Showcase the "Most Active College" or "Top Campus Ambassador" to drive inter-college competition.

## 3. Growth Channels

### Campus Ambassador Program (CAP)
- **Scale to 500+ Colleges**: Use the `/ambassadors` route to recruit and manage student leads.
- **Gamified Rewards**: Tier-based rewards (Bronze, Silver, Gold) with perks like VIP passes, internships, and WeFest merch.

### Strategic Partnerships
- **Sponsor Network**: Leverage the `/sponsors` hub to cross-promote brands that students love (RedBull, OnePlus, Unstop, etc.).
- **College Tie-ups**: Become the official ticketing partner for major fests like Mood Indigo, Antaragni, or Rendezvous.

## 4. Email & Lifecycle Marketing

- **Organizer Newsletter**: Weekly tips on managing events and maximizing ticket sales.
- **Student Digest**: Personalized event recommendations based on their interests and college.
- **Retention**: Re-engagement emails for users who haven't explored events in 30 days.

## 5. Analytics & Performance Tracking

- **Conversion Tracking**: Monitor the funnel from "Landing Page" to "Ticket Purchased".
- **Source Attribution**: Identify which channels (Instagram, CAP, Search) are driving the most traffic.
- **User Heatmaps**: Analyze how users interact with the event discovery marketplace to optimize UI.
